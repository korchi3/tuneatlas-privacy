Privacy Policy for TuneAtlas

Effective date: May 10, 2026

TuneAtlas is a worldwide radio streaming mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

TuneAtlas does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

Online Radio Streaming

TuneAtlas streams online radio content from internet radio sources. Radio stations and stream providers may process technical information such as your IP address when you connect to their streams.

Advertising

TuneAtlas may display advertisements using Google AdMob or Google Mobile Ads. These services may collect and use certain information such as device information, advertising identifiers, app interactions, diagnostics, approximate location, and advertising performance data to provide, measure, and improve advertising.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Some data may be processed by third-party services such as Google AdMob for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, and service improvement.

Children's Privacy

TuneAtlas is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since TuneAtlas does not directly create user accounts or store personal user profiles, we do not retain account-based personal data. For privacy questions or deletion requests related to the app, you may contact us using the email below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
